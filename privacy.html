Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

RemindMe respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we do and do not collect, how your data is handled, and the choices you have. Our goal is simple: keep your data private and on your devices.

No Personal Data Collection

RemindMe does not collect, store, or process personal data on our servers. We do not track you, profile you, or build advertising identifiers. The app contains no third‑party analytics SDKs or advertising SDKs.

On‑Device Data

Your reminders, settings, and usage stay on your device. Synchronization between iPhone and Apple Watch is handled by Apple’s system frameworks. We do not have access to the contents of your reminders.

Network Access

Apart from the connections required by Apple’s services for notifications and device communication, RemindMe does not send your data to external servers controlled by us.

Purchase Information

If the app offers in‑app purchases or subscriptions, transactions are processed by Apple. We do not receive your payment details. Apple may provide us with anonymous sales reports that contain no personal information.

Children’s Privacy

RemindMe is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

Because we do not collect or store personal data, there is nothing for us to access, correct, or delete. You can delete all app data at any time by removing the app from your device.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we will update this page and revise the “Last updated” date above.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Contact us at support@remind.info.
